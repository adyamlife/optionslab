4Signal Alignment Scoring — compute_signal_alignment()
Called once per candidate  ·  weights = get_sub_weight(factor, sub, regime)
effective_max = applicable weight sum CHANGED
A check enters effective_max when the structure has a scoring rule for it — regardless of whether the signal value was positive, negative, or in a neutral zone. Only checks where no rule exists for this structure are excluded entirely.
Fires positive — score +wt, effective_max +wt, appears in contributions[]
Fires negative — score −wt, effective_max +wt, appears in contributions[]
Neutral zone — score +0, effective_max +wt, silent (no UI noise)
Not applicable — score +0, effective_max unchanged

5Ranking & Decision Gates — select_structure()
Candidates sorted by pct descending. Thresholds now read from scoring.toml [gates] NEW rather than hardcoded.
